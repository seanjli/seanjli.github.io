Hi, I'm Sean.

I am freshman and prospective Course 18 and 6-3 at MIT. My research interests are in algebra, number theory, and enumerative combinatorics. I have done research at MIT PRIMES-USA under Dr. Tanya Khovanova and the University of Virginia REU under Prof. Ken Ono, and I am an incoming researcher at the University of Minnesota-Duluth REU under Prof. Joseph Gallian.

I am also an active member of MIT DanceTroupe, MIT Asian Dance Team, the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament, and the Undergraduate Math Association. In my free time, I enjoy baking bread, going on walks along the Charles River, and fermenting in Hayden Library.
