Sample Materials

Here are some samples of materials that I've created.

  • Intro to Functional Equations (pdf). A handout introducing what to do (and what not to do) when faced with a functional equation. Accompanied a two-hour lecture at Youth EUCLID.


  • Proposed Problem Collection (pdf). A repository of 120 problems that I've proposed to contests.


  • States and Recursion (WIP) (pdf). Provides an introduction to solving problems via recursion probabilistically and in terms of expected value.
