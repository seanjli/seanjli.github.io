Teaching


Youth EUCLID Association

In 2019, I taught for our two top Math Practice program: our Math Practice 4.0 class (to prepare for advanced AMC 10/12 concepts) and our Math Practice 5.0 class (an introduction to national and international olympiads). I also teach AMC-level winter and summer camps.

Our website can be found here.


STEM Academy

I currently am a mentor for the Math Advanced 5.0 program, where students well-versed in the basics of olympiad math learn new techniques and methods to clinch top spots in the USA national olympiads. I've also taught material for the computational AMC series. I've produced many olympiad and MOP qualifiers.

Sample Materials

Here are some samples of materials that I've created.

  • Intro to FEs (pdf). A handout introducing what to do (and what not to do) when faced with an FE. Accompanied a two-hour lecture at YEA.
  • Proposed Problem Collection (pdf). A repository of 120 problems that I've proposed to contests.
  • States and Recursion (WIP) (pdf). Provides an introduction to solving problems via recursion probabilistically and in terms of expected value.
  • Roots of a Polynomial (pdf). Serves as a comprehensive overview to calculate symmetric expressions regarding the roots of a polynomial.
